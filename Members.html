Founder Members

Dr. Dnyaneshwar Waghchoure

President

Dr.Dnyaneshwar Waghchoure is an international agriculture expert & well known in the area of Biotechnology, agriculture, crop protection & organic input production, processing of Herbal products & Marketing area. He is basically biologist completed his PG in Entomology and Ph.D in Nematology. Dr. Waghchoure has developed specialty biotech products in organic management of pest & diseases in domestic market. Apart from this, he has designed 23 human health care Ayurveda products, 5 Poultry medicine for organic farming & several food products including spices & herbal tea. The Basic scope of Specialty of Dr. Waghchoure is Bio-pesticides and Bio-fertilizer manufacturing, medicinal pant cultivation, processing and value addition. Sugarcane signal eye bud nursery technology, formulation of herbal medicine, crop cultivation and protection, livestock frame development, herbal poultry bird production, food processing, plant growth promoter & micronutrients are some of areas of his specialization. Moreover expertise in development of horticulture garden, farmhouse project, agro ecotourism, agriculture automation project, Bio energy production & mushroom Production are also the fields of his expertise. He is founder of Ashwamedh Group of Industries & Ashwamedh herbal Research & Development Trust. For the Knowledge of basic agricultural practices & crop productions Ashwamedh has started monthly Magazine Ashwamedh krushidoot edited by Dr. Dnyaneshwar Waghchoure

He is working with several International and National Social & Scientific Institutes and NGOs. He is the recipient of several awards offered by several organisation & government. He has published several international paper in reputed journal & Guided two Ph.D students.

Dr. Ramnath Jagtap

Vice President

Dr. Ramnath Jagtap, being born in agriculturist family has completed his post-graduation in Plant Pathology from MPKV, Rahuri and done his Ph.D in bio efficacy of different fungicides in Powdery Mildew of Grapes. He has published five research papers till to date and having in depth knowledge of bio control agents. He established Krishidoot Bio Herbals an ISO 9001:2008 company for manufacturing organic inputs in year 2000. Company is backed up by its strong R&D team and well equipped Analytical Laboratory & own lands and polyhouse for R&D purpose. He is consulting the farming community through field visits, soil testing reports, microscopic examinations of the plant and suggesting suitable remedies for pest control and soil fertility problems. He has developed his expertise through his long endeavored journey of over 25 years.

He believes in “All culture will die tomorrow, but Agriculture never”, “customer first, Reputation first”. The company has strong hold in domestic and international market. Last but not least he has solutions to each & every problems in Agriculture.
