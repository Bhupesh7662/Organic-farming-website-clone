Functioning

AMMA - AGRICULTURAL MICRO-ORGANISMS MANUFACTURERS & FARMERS ASSOCIATION

For carrying out its activities, the Association is assisted by Advisory Committees in different disciplines, viz.,

  • Bio pesticide registration and licensing
  • Environment protection
  • Marketing skill development
  • Policy, Finance & Taxation
  • Agricultural Sciences

Each Advisory Committee comprises 7 -25 senior experts/heads of respective disciplines from member organizations and academic institutions. Each Advisory Committee is normally headed by a member of the FAI Board to provide proper linkage and ensure consistency in approach to various issues. This helps in formulation of suitable strategies to deal with various problems.
