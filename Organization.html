AMMA - AGRICULTURAL MICRO-ORGANISMS MANUFACTURERS & FARMERS ASSOCIATION

The Agricultural Micro Organisms Manufacturers and Farmers Association ( AMMA) is a non-profit organization representing organic agro inputs mainly Biopesticide ,Biofertiliser, plant growth promoting rhizobacteria and botanical pesticides Manufacturers, Farmers, Technocrats, Scientists, Exporters in India. The purpose of association is to take effective steps for registration of Bio pesticides, Bio fertilisers, botanicals and organic product in India & other countries and most important “Promotion of Organic Farming in India” the vision of Hon. Prime Minister Shri. Narendra Modiji , Govt.. Of India.

AMMA association has entered in MOU with Mahatma Phule Krishi Vidhyapeeth, Rahuri , Navsari Agriculture University, Navsari ,Junagadh Agriculture University, Junagadh and Central Agriculture University, Imphal for generating bio efficacy data & developing latest technologies in Organic product manufacturing as well as arranging training programmes for Industry, Technocrats & students.

AMMA, central agriculture & state agriculture universities are jointly developing bio-efficacy & toxicological data ( liquid formulation) on following strains of biopesticides and signed MOU for following products in first phase

Increased use of chemical fertilizer & hazardous pesticides in cultivation lead to the declining productivity of Indian Agriculture is now become the main concern in farming community as well as among scientific community. The use of bio pesticides & bio fertilizers become the only solution to solve those recurrent problems in todays agriculture.

The members of AMMA association have well equipped manufacturing facility as per Govt. Standards across India and capacity to manufacture organic input needed for Export Quality Agricultural Produce and meeting the demand & supply need of organic agricultural inputs.

The Association was concepted in Nov 2015 with an objective to bring together all concerned with bio pesticides, fertilizers, plant growth promoting rhizobacteria, botanical and organic products manufacturers in view to:

Assist the industry in improving its operative efficiency.

  • To take initiative for bio efficacy , phytotoxicity, DNA studies, toxicology studies and shelf life studies of microbial products to register in central insecticides board Faridabad.
  • To assist products developed by members and take leads to register botanical pesticides and fungicides in central insecticides board Faridabad.
  • To collect store and conserve microbial strains and supply to the stakeholders after proper technical know how regarding microbial strains.
  • To supply strains to the students for studies
  • To make awareness in the farmers, producers, agriculture dept. and policy makers about effectiveness of Micro-organisms based Bio-Inputs.
  • Find solutions to the problems facing the organic industry and agriculture.
  • Promotion of Bio pesticides, Bio fertilizers and organic products for organic farming as well as conventional farming.

Microbial strains available with AMMA association

  • Bacillus subtilis
  • Ampelomyces quisqualis
  • Trichoderma viride
  • Trichoderma harzianum
  • Pseudomonas fluoroscens

Ensuring Food Security through Integrated Pest Management (IPM) & Integrated Plant Nutrient Management (IPNS) is the prime objective of the Association.

The AMMA is agro based group of highly experienced Technical and Professional Members supporting farmers and government and also updating technology for agriculture growth in India.
