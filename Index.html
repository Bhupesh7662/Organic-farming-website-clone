AGRICULTURAL MICRO-ORGANISMS MANUFACTURERS & FARMERS ASSOCIATION (AMMA)

AMMA is a non-profit organization representing mainly Biopesticide & Biofertiliser Manufacturers, Farmers, Technocrats, Scientists, Exporters in India.The purpose of association is to take effective steps for registration of Bio pesticides, Bio fertilisers and organic product in India & other countries and most important “Promotion of Organic Farming in India” the vision of Hon. Prime Minister Narendra Modi , Govt.. Of India. .
